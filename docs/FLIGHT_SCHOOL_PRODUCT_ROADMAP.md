# Flight School Product Roadmap

> Last updated: 2026-05-01
> Product: EASA_app
> Focus: Flight schools, ATOs, and pilot training organisations

## Positioning Goal

Build EASA_app to be the daily operating system for flight schools, not just a document control tool. The product should connect manuals, training delivery, student acknowledgements, instructor workflows, and EASA compliance in one place.

## Product Promises

- Faster to adopt than legacy aviation manual systems
- Easier for students and instructors to use every day
- More directly useful for training operations, not only compliance

## Target Personas

### Head of Training

- Needs confidence that students and instructors are working from the latest approved material
- Wants a clear view of pending reads, overdue acknowledgements, and training-impacting changes

### Chief Flight Instructor / Instructor

- Needs lesson-linked documents, briefings, and sign-offs
- Wants fast access to what changed and what matters for today’s training

### Compliance Manager / Accountable Manager

- Needs audit trails, approvals, regulation mappings, and traceable revision history
- Wants to know what changed in EASA and which school documents need action

### Student Pilot

- Needs a simple mobile reading experience
- Wants assigned reading, clear change summaries, and confidence that study material is current

## MVP Wedge

Win small and midsize ATOs by solving four jobs better than enterprise manual platforms:

- Publish and distribute controlled manuals quickly
- Turn document changes into training actions
- Track who has read and acknowledged what
- Show which EASA changes affect the school and where

## Phase 1: Core Launch

### Product Outcomes

- Replace scattered PDFs, Word files, and email updates
- Provide a trusted source of current manuals and notices
- Give admins a basic approval and audit workflow

### Features

- Structured manual hub with revisions and approvals
- Role-based portal for students, instructors, and admins
- Read-and-acknowledge flow for critical documents
- Mobile-friendly manual reader
- Import pipeline for Word and PDF manuals
- Change center with:
  - what changed
  - why it changed
  - who must read it
  - what action is required
- Basic compliance mapping between EASA source material and manual sections

### Success Metrics

- First manual imported in under 1 day
- First revision published in under 30 minutes after setup
- 90%+ acknowledgement completion for assigned critical updates

## Phase 2: Training Operations Layer

### Product Outcomes

- Make the app useful in daily training, not only during audits
- Tie documents directly to lesson delivery and school processes

### Features

- Lesson-linked reading packs
- Phase-of-training document assignment
- Instructor sign-offs and student confirmations
- Standardised training forms and evaluations
- Course dashboards by programme, phase, and cohort
- Change impact view showing which lessons, syllabi, or SOPs are affected

### Success Metrics

- Instructors use the system weekly, not just compliance staff
- Schools assign training documents by phase or lesson instead of sending files manually

## Phase 3: Compliance Intelligence

### Product Outcomes

- Reduce manual compliance analysis work
- Help schools respond faster to EASA changes with better explainability

### Features

- Regulation change monitoring with plain-language summaries
- AI-assisted search over approved manuals only
- Suggested compliance mappings
- Suggested revision text with human approval required
- Audit-ready evidence packs
- Risk-based queues:
  - must update
  - likely update
  - watchlist

### Success Metrics

- Reduced time from regulation update to internal action
- Higher mapping coverage between regulations and internal manuals

## Phase 4: Expansion

### Product Outcomes

- Support larger ATOs and multi-base schools
- Create stronger retention through operational depth

### Features

- Multi-campus and multi-AOC style controls where needed
- Advanced permissions and delegation
- Integrations with LMS, student records, and messaging tools
- Branded student portals
- Benchmarking and health analytics across document engagement

## What Not To Prioritise Early

- Deep airline or MRO workflows
- Heavy XML-first authoring
- Enterprise-only contract features before core usability is strong
- Complex workflow builders before standard ATO templates are excellent

## Product Strategy Summary

Web Manuals is strong at document control. EASA_app should win by connecting document control to how flight schools actually train people.
