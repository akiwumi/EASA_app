## Problem Section

### Heading

Most flight schools still manage critical updates across PDFs, Word files, email, and memory

### Body

That makes training inconsistent, acknowledgements hard to track, and audits more stressful than they need to be. EASA_app brings manuals, revision control, student reading, and compliance workflows into one system.

## Outcome Grid

### Card 1

Publish once, distribute instantly

Push approved updates to instructors, students, and staff from one controlled source of truth.

### Card 2

Connect manuals to training delivery

Assign reading by lesson, phase, aircraft, or programme so the right people see the right procedures at the right time.

### Card 3

See what changed and who must act

Every update shows what changed, why it matters, who must read it, and what action is required.
