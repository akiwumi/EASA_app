# EASA PPL(A) Theory Training Programme

Last updated: 2026-05-06

## Purpose

This is a starter training programme for the app's `training_programmes`, `training_phases`, and `training_lessons` model.

It is designed for:

- EASA PPL(A) theoretical knowledge
- classroom-led delivery
- flight-school onboarding and demo data

It is based on the source materials already stored in this repository:

- `books/ATO PPL Appendix A Distance theory 2025-03-05.md`
- `books/ATO PPL Appendix B In-class theory Rev 4 2025-03-05.md`

## Programme Summary

- Programme code: `EASA-PPLA-THEORY`
- Programme name: `EASA PPL(A) Theory`
- Delivery mode: `ground`
- Total planned hours: `100`
- Total lessons: `29`

## Phases

### Phase 1. Regulatory and Technical Foundations

Focus:

- air law and licensing rules
- aircraft systems fundamentals
- human performance

Subjects included:

- Air Law and ATC Procedures
- Aircraft General Knowledge
- Aircraft General Knowledge Instrumentation
- Human Performance and Limitations

Planned hours: `28`

### Phase 2. Planning, Performance, and Operating Environment

Focus:

- aircraft loading and performance
- planning and navigation
- weather and operating procedures

Subjects included:

- Mass and Balance
- Flight Performance
- Flight Planning
- Meteorology
- General Navigation
- Radio Navigation
- Operational Procedures

Planned hours: `56`

### Phase 3. Flight Principles and Communications

Focus:

- aerodynamic understanding
- radio communication readiness
- final theory consolidation

Subjects included:

- Principles of Flight
- VFR Communications

Planned hours: `16`
