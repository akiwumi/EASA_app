# Flight School Pricing Draft

> Last updated: 2026-05-01
> Product: EASA_app
> Audience: Flight schools and ATOs

## Pricing Strategy

Price EASA_app like a modern school platform, not an enterprise procurement project. The goal is to make adoption easy for small and midsize ATOs while still leaving room for expansion revenue.

## Principles

- Keep pricing simple and visible
- Avoid long mandatory commitments for normal schools
- Bundle enough student access to remove friction
- Charge for admin and operational value, not reader anxiety
- Make onboarding feel included, not punitive

## Proposed Packages

### Starter ATO

- `EUR 199/month`
- Best for new or smaller schools

Includes:

- 3 admin/editor users
- 40 student users
- 10 instructor users
- Manual hub and revision control
- Read-and-acknowledge workflows
- Mobile reader
- Basic reporting
- 1 training programme

### Growth ATO

- `EUR 549/month`
- Best for growing schools with multiple cohorts or programmes

Includes:

- 8 admin/editor users
- 150 student users
- 35 instructor users
- Everything in Starter
- Compliance mapping
- Lesson-linked documents
- Change impact dashboard
- Audit export pack
- Branded portal
- Priority onboarding

### Multi-Base ATO

- `Custom pricing`
- Best for larger schools, multi-base operations, or advanced governance needs

Includes:

- Unlimited or high-volume seat model
- Multi-campus permissions
- Advanced approval controls
- Integration support
- Dedicated onboarding and migration support

## Optional Add-Ons

### AI Copilot

- `EUR 99/month` and up

Includes:

- Manual search in plain language
- Change summaries
- Suggested draft updates

### Extra Student Packs

- `EUR 39/month per 50 students`

### White-Glove Migration

- One-time setup fee based on document complexity

Includes:

- Manual import
- Structure cleanup
- Initial mapping workshop

## Packaging Logic

The base value should come from controlled manuals and acknowledgements. The upgrade path should come from operational usefulness:

- Starter buys control
- Growth buys training workflows and compliance depth
- Enterprise buys governance and scale

## Competitive Angle Vs Web Manuals

The public Web Manuals pricing model is more enterprise-shaped, with a higher entry point, added compliance-library costs, paid training, and longer commitments on upper plans. EASA_app should differentiate with:

- lower entry cost
- shorter commitments
- clearer total cost
- more student-friendly packaging
- more included onboarding at mid-tier

## Commercial Notes

- Offer annual billing with 10 to 15 percent discount
- Keep monthly billing available
- Run a 60-day pilot offer for schools migrating from PDF or Word processes
- Let schools expand student counts without re-negotiating the whole contract

## Example Sales Message

EASA_app gives a flight school controlled manuals, student acknowledgements, and EASA-linked training workflows without forcing them into an enterprise buying cycle.
