**Headline:**
Most flight schools still manage critical updates across PDFs, Word files, email, and memory.

**Body:**
That makes training inconsistent, acknowledgements impossible to verify, and audits more stressful than they need to be. When an EASA change lands, there's no single system that connects the regulation, the manual, the instructor, and the student. EASA_app is that system.

## Outcomes (3 Cards)

**Card 1: Publish once, distribute instantly**
Push approved manual updates to instructors, students, and staff from one controlled source of truth. No forwarding emails. No version confusion.

**Card 2: Connect manuals to training delivery**
Assign reading by lesson, phase, aircraft type, or programme. The right people see the right procedures at the right time — not after the lesson has already happened.

**Card 3: See what changed and who must act**
Every update shows what changed, why it matters, who must read it, and what action is required — before the next audit, not during it.

## Features

**Section headline:**
Built for the way ATOs actually work.

1. **Controlled Manuals with Revision History** — Publish and version every training document with a full change log. Roll back with one click.

2. **EASA Change Monitoring** — RSS feeds, source snapshots, and AI-powered impact analysis surface what matters before it becomes a compliance gap.

3. **AI Impact Assessment** — When a regulation changes, the AI flags which sections of your manuals are affected and generates a plain-language summary for your review.

4. **Human-Led Approval Workflow** — AI helps identify impact and draft updates, but every change is reviewed and approved by your compliance team. Full rollback history included.

5. **Read-and-Acknowledge Workflows** — Assign reading to instructors and students with tracked acknowledgements. Know exactly who has read, who hasn't, and when.

6. **Lesson-Linked Documents and Training Forms** — Attach manuals and procedures directly to lessons or training phases so content is always contextually available.

7. **Mobile-Friendly Document Access** — Students and instructors can read, acknowledge, and sign off on mobile — no desktop required.

8. **Role-Based Access** — Compliance managers, instructors, and students each see what's relevant to them without a cluttered interface.

9. **Audit-Ready Reporting** — Export your approval history, acknowledgement records, and compliance mappings in a format inspectors expect.

10. **Multi-Programme Support** — Manage ATPL, PPL, IR, and type rating programmes in parallel, each with their own manual sets and reading assignments.

## How It Works (4-Step Workflow)

**Step 01 — Monitor EASA Changes**
Regulatory updates from EASA are automatically detected, summarised by AI, and surfaced in your compliance queue. Nothing slips through unnoticed.

**Step 02 — Review and Approve Manual Impact**
Your team reviews the AI's recommended changes side-by-side with your existing manual text. Approve, edit, or reject — with a full note and history trail.

**Step 03 — Assign Reading by Lesson and Role**
Distribute approved updates to the right instructors and students based on training programme, lesson stage, or aircraft type.

**Step 04 — Track Acknowledgements and Sign-Offs**
See exactly who has read, acknowledged, and completed required action. Know your training record is clean before the next lesson — or the next audit.

## Testing Notes

| Element | Priority | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| Hero headline | High | First thing read — biggest impact on bounce rate |
| Hero CTAs | High | Direct conversion driver |
| Problem headline | Medium | Sets the frame for whether the reader self-identifies |
| Closing CTA headline | Medium | Last persuasion moment before exit or conversion |
| Subheadline | Medium | Determines whether skimmers keep reading |
| Outcome cards | Low | Reinforces value, rarely the deciding factor alone |
| Features headline | Low | Section label — low individual conversion weight |
| Competitive section | Low | Trust-builder, rarely tested in isolation |

**Recommended first test:** Hero headline (Control A vs Variant B — pain-first). Single variable, highest traffic section, fastest to call.

**Recommended second test:** Hero CTAs (Control A vs Variant D — value-first primary). Tests whether lowering the commitment of the primary CTA increases total demo bookings.
